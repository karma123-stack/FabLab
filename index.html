Welcome to CST FabLAB

Innovation & Creativity

What we do at CST FabLab

FabLab CST was inaugurated on 25th August 2022 in the College of Science and Technology, Bhutan. CST FabLab is an open platform for learning and innovating new ideas. You can learn various skills and work on your projects here. It's the place where your ideas come to life. CST FabLab is located at Rinchendeng in the College of Science and Technology.

Vision

“Provide Digital Design Tools to Ignite a Culture of Innovation”

“Inspire the Future of Learning and Creating”

Our services

Our FabLab student management will guide you to build your ideas to reality

Custom Prototyping

Bring your ideas to life with our cutting-edge prototyping services. Whether it's a simple model or a complex design, we provide the tools and expertise to turn your concepts into reality.

Workshop

Unlock your potential with our hands-on workshops which is guided by student body training. Learn the latest in fabrication technology, and gain the skills to build anything you can imagine.

Community Services

Our student management team helps the community by providing their serices, we help students with disabilities by building them a assistive technology which helps them to live independently.

Orientation

We Orientates student of our college so that intrested ones can participate in our fablab activities. we teach basic skills to them like python gives brief discription about fablab and its machines.

Operating Machines

The student management team teaches the intrested student on how to operate the machines so that their ideas can be developed to a prototype, after guides them to be Productive.

Products

The student management team produces a commerical Products for their lab's finance. We take orders and builds them and deliverys them to the customer.
